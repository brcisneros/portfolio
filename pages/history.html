WHO'S BRIAN?

Brian is the name I respond to -I mean, that's the name I've had since I was born, why wouldn't I?-, Cisneros being my family name. I have a middle name and a second surename but that would make this text unnecessarily long -aah the irony-. Anyways, I was born in a little town in Argentina called Guatraché, in the province of La Pampa, but I grew up 50km from there in Bernasconi (another teeny tiny town).

Fast forward 28 years and I'm currently living in the portuary city of Bahía Blanca, in Buenos Aires province, separated (it sucks I know) and with a 4 years old beautiful girl. Struggling to find and keep regular jobs but studiyng web developing in hopes to find a better future for myself and my daughter.
